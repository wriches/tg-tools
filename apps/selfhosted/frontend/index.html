tg-tools

A small suite of Telegram utilities.

Before you start

This tool acts through your Telegram account using an unofficial API client. It can remove people from groups and send direct messages to people who aren't your contacts — both carry a real risk of Telegram rate-limiting or banning your account. You are responsible for using it within Telegram's Terms of Service and applicable laws. Send conservatively.

Find groups you share with a Telegram user, remove them where you can, and message the admins of the rest to ask. All actions are real and require confirmation.
